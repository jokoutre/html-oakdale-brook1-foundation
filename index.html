About Us

Welcome to Oakdale Brook Foundation, a beacon of hope and guidance in the journey of home education and youth mentorship. Founded on the pillars of faith, mentorship, and community support, we are committed to nurturing a generation of young individuals equipped with a strong sense of identity and purpose. Our mission transcends the boundaries of traditional education, as we strive to preserve generations and redeem children's destinies through comprehensive discipleship, mentorship, and unwavering family support. Join us in our quest to lay a foundation for a life filled with discovery, purpose, and empowerment.

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of young people distinguished by their robust identity and resolute purpose.

Our Mission

To safeguard future generations and transform children's destinies through deep-rooted discipleship, dedicated mentorship, and extensive family support.

Our Core Values

The essence of our culture at Oakdale Brook is encapsulated in our ABCDE - Accountability, Birthing new ideas, Connections, Documentation of our journeys, and Empowerment of the youth, parents, and mentors. We are committed to excellence, innovation, building meaningful relationships, chronicling our shared experiences, and fostering growth and fulfillment within our community.

Homeschool Courses

Dive into the heart of home education with our flagship Homeschool Start-Up Course. Crafted by the Oakdale Brook Foundation, in collaboration with Advantages East Africa and Rhema Schools, this 4-week journey is designed to illuminate the path for families embarking on or rekindling their homeschooling journey. Explore the essence of home education, understand your child's unique learning styles, navigate through the six core methods of home education, select the right curriculum, and set up your homeschool with confidence and clarity.

Who Should Enroll?

Whether you're stepping into homeschooling with faith and boldness but seeking guidance, feeling uncertain about your curriculum choice, or a seasoned educator eager for fresh insights, this course is your gateway to a deeper understanding and renewed confidence in home education.

Course Structure

Led by seasoned homeschooling mothers, this online course offers weekly 2-hour sessions filled with rich insights, practical advice, and supportive community engagement. Equip yourself with the knowledge, skills, and tools needed for a fruitful and rewarding homeschooling experience.

Home Education Conversations

Engage in meaningful dialogue and community support through our bi-monthly Home Education Conversations. Every first and third Saturday of the month, join us and special guests as we explore pertinent home education themes, drawn from the diverse experiences and needs within our homeschooling community. These sessions are designed to be a safe space for sharing insights, asking questions, and discovering new resources and strategies to enhance your homeschooling experience.
