Empowering Youth Through Mentorship

At Oakdale, we believe in empowering young people by providing them with the tools, guidance, and support to navigate life’s challenges. Our Youth Mentorship Program is designed to nurture talents, build strong character, and equip youth with essential life skills.

What Is Our Youth Mentorship Program

Our program focuses on holistic development, helping young people excel academically, spiritually, and socially. Through one-on-one mentorship and group workshops, we provide the support they need to thrive.
Character Building:Instilling strong moral values, integrity, and self-discipline.
Leadership Development:Equipping youth to become responsible, forward-thinking leaders.
Life Skills Training:Teaching practical skills, from financial literacy to time management, to prepare for adulthood.
Spiritual Guidance:Helping youth grow in faith, rooted in strong Christian values.

How the Mentorship Program Works

Our program operates through a combination of one-on-one mentorship, group workshops, and community service projects. Youth are matched with mentors based on their interests and goals, ensuring personalized guidance and support.
One-on-One Mentorship:Each youth is paired with a mentor for personalized guidance.
Group Workshops:Interactive workshops focusing on leadership, career planning, and life skills.
Community Service:Encouraging youth to give back through service projects and volunteerism.
