Meet Our Facilitators

Gloria Brown

Gloria Brown is Directior of School Choice and Co-Founder of ADVANTAGES Middle East. Gloria has been a home educator, organizee and advocate for alternative education for 30 years. In addition to her role with ADVANTAGES,she remains a highly influential leader in the home education community: organizing community coops, teaching, and consulting. A champion of school choice, Gloria is an expert at assisting families with curriculum strategies. In addition to her advocacy, Gloria serves as President of the Abu Dhabi Homeschool Association and Director of the Abu Dhabi Homeschool Coop. She is a mother of 5 and grandmother of 5. She resides in Abu Dhabi with her husband and two of their sons.
